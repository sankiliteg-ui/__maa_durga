My Own Thoughts
For My Bestie 💌
Seedha dil se — sirf tere liye, Annu jaan!
01
🌸 Dosti Hi Shakti Hai
Annu, you know what? Real friendship is just like Maa Durga — powerful, pure, and unconditional. You have always been there with your sweet smile and warm heart. That energy you carry? That's the real Navratri energy — nine days of goddess power wrapped in one girl called ANNU!
"Dosti woh noor hai jo andhere mein bhi raah dikhati hai — aur teri dosti mere liye wahi noor hai." 🪔
02
🌟 Tu Meri Festival Hai
Festivals come once a year — but you bring celebration into every ordinary day. Talking to you, laughing with you, sharing those random thoughts — Annu, honestly these moments feel bigger than any festival. Navratri is special, but your friendship is a blessing all year long!
"Tu mere zindagi ka wo tyohaar hai jo kabhi khatam nahi hota." ✨
03
💚 Hamesha Strong Rehna
Navratri teaches us that every form of Maa Durga faces challenges — but she never gives up. Annu, I want you to remember this always: no matter what life throws at you, you are stronger than you think. You are that flame that keeps burning bright even in the harshest wind!
"Mushkilein aayengi, lekin tujh mein jo Shakti hai woh kabhi nahi bujhegi." 🔥
04
💜 Teri Khwahishein Poori Hon
This Navratri, I pray to Maa Durga with a full heart that every dream you have comes true. Every wish, every goal, every little thing you want — may Maa herself clear the path for you. Because Annu, you deserve the very best this world can offer. You truly do!
"Teri khwahishein itni pyari hain jitni tu khud hai — Maa zaroor poori karegi." 🌺
05
🧡 Sabse Pyari Mitar
God gives us family — but best friends are chosen, and choosing you as my mitar is one of the best things that ever happened. You are sweet, kind, funny and one of the most beautiful souls I know. Thank you for being YOU, Annu. Don't ever change! 🌸
"Tu meri best friend nahi, tu meri sabse pyari zindagi ki dua hai." 💛
